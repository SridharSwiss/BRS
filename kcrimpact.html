Decade-by-decade view since independence (high level)

1950s–1960s

Hyderabad as fiscal anchor; Telangana’s revenue strength contrasted within the merged state; integration dynamics shaped allocations.

1970s–1980s

Urban services concentrated in Hyderabad; regional investment gaps persisted, laying groundwork for future demands.

1990s–2000s

Liberalization boosted IT/services; distribution debates intensified, strengthening the economic case for statehood.

2010s

Post-2014 formation: outperformance vs national averages on GSDP and PCI; capex-forward approach; services dominance with agri/industry lift.

2020s

Services-led resilience; manageable debt metrics; revenue surplus re-emerging post-pandemic; leadership transition in late 2023.

Quick take: Scale up fast, incomes up, services lead. Infrastructure-first stance and strong tax mobilization underpin resilience.
